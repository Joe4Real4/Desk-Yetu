❓ Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting started with Desk Yetu.

How long does setup take?
▼

Most businesses are up and running within 30 minutes. We connect to your existing WhatsApp Business number, import your product catalog (from Excel or Google Sheets), and configure your price lists. Our team walks you through it via WhatsApp call — no tech expertise needed.

Do my customers need to install anything?
▼

Absolutely not. Your customers continue messaging you on WhatsApp exactly as they do now. Desk Yetu works behind the scenes — reading messages, generating quotes, and sending updates — all through the same WhatsApp chat they already use.

How does M-Pesa integration work?
▼

We integrate with Safaricom's M-Pesa API. When a customer pays, the payment is automatically matched to their order using the M-Pesa confirmation code. On the Growth and Pro plans, you can also send STK push requests directly from the system — the customer gets a payment prompt on their phone instantly.

Can I use this for multiple branches?
▼

Yes — the Pro plan supports unlimited branches with centralized inventory management. Each branch can have its own stock levels, drivers, and staff, while head office sees consolidated reports. Perfect for distributors with warehouses in Nairobi, Mombasa, and Kisumu.

What happens after the 14-day trial?
▼

After your free pilot, you choose a plan that fits your business. There's no automatic billing — we ask first. Your data stays intact, and you can export everything if you decide not to continue. Most businesses see ROI within the first week of the trial.

Is my data secure?
▼

Your data is stored on secure servers with bank-level encryption. We never share customer information with third parties. All M-Pesa transactions are processed through Safaricom's secure API. We also offer data backup and export features so you always have control.
